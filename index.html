About us

As the market becomes more competitive, more and more companies ask for candidates with previous hands-on experience and paid internships are the best way to do that. More importantly, internships put you at an advantage when applying for jobs. Entrepreneurship Cell of CCA, NIT Durgapur, makes it easier for you to get these internships with our Intern-NIT. The Intern-NIT brings to you new and exhilarating paid internships to keep you busy over the summer. Students from NIT Durgapur and other esteemed colleges across India can apply for paid internship roles offered by the companies. Internship offered will be for Summer 2019. All year students can apply.
